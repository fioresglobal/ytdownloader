Welcome to Map-Area Calculator, your go-to online tool for accurately measuring and calculating areas in square meters directly from maps. Whether you're a land surveyor, urban planner, real estate professional, or simply curious about the size of a specific area, our user-friendly platform makes it easy to obtain precise measurements by selecting points on a map.

How to Use

Enter your home adress or cordinates of your house and find it in the map. Secondly select the polygon symbol from the right corner of the map. Pick the edges of your roof and click finish. It will give you the area of your houses rooftop in meter square.
